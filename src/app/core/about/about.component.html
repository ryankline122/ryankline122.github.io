About Me

Hello World, my name is Ryan Kline, I am a full-stack software developer based out of West Michigan specializing in web, mobile, and IoT products. I graduated from Grand Valley State University in December of 2023 with my Bachelor's of Science degree in Computer Science, and have been working in the industry ever since.

Since breaking into the industry with my first internship in 2022, I've had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of projects ranging from automated test suites for an autonomous robot, a web-based member portal for large health care provider, a data center management system for a global furniture manufacturer, and a cloud-based back-end of an innovative Parking Access and Revenue Control product. This breadth of experience so early in my career puts me in a unique position to confidently tackle whatever project comes my way.

This site serves as a place for me to showcase my skills, side projects, and professional journey. I will also be using this as a space to share ideas, insights, and discoveries in the form of blog posts (coming soon). Whether you're here to explore my past projects or to get my thoughts on the latest trends in the industry, I hope you'll find inspiration and value in my work.
